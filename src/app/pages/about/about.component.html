Our Mission:
Helping you understand yourself better

Our goal is to empower you to gain valuable insights into your mood and energy patterns. By reflecting on your daily experiences, we believe you can make positive changes and improve your overall well-being.

We take your privacy seriously. Your data is stored securely on your device and is never shared with anyone else. Tracking your mood is a personal journey and we’re here to support you every step.
